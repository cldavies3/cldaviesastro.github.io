Outreach Activities

As an ordinary member of the Institute of Physics (IOP) South West Branch committee (September 2017 to present), I am involved in the organisation and orchestration of Physics-related events in the South West region. In May 2018, I organised the IOP's involvement in Exeter Pride, the local LGBTQ+ festival and largest such event in Devon. This was the first time that a science-based event had taken place in conjunction with the festival.

I am also involved with the following upcoming astronomy-themed event within the Exeter region

  • Star gazing event in the St Leonards and Newtown area of Exeter (week beginning February 11 2019).

    • I have experience giving scientific talks to general public audiences of a wide range of ages on subjects including how stars and their planets form, the Rosetta comet-landing mission, and the astronomical field of interferometry. I also have 6+ years of experience giving planetarium and open-air guided tours of the sky.

    If you would like to contact me regarding the topics of my talks or to arrange for me to visit your group/society etc, please use the contact details at the bottom of this page.
