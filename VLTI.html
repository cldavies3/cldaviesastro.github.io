Very Large Telescope Interferometer (VLTI)

The GRAVITY instrument

I have had PI proposals for observations using the GRAVITY instrument of the VLTI accepted in P60 (science verification), P98, P99, P100 and P101. These have all been conducted in service mode meaning that the observations themselves were conducted by VLTI staff astronomers. The VLTI is situated in the mountains of Paranal, Chile.

GRAVITY combines the light (K-band) from the four auxiliary telescopes or the four unit telescopes of the VLTI. For further information on the GRAVITY instrument, see ESO's instrument-specific webpages.
