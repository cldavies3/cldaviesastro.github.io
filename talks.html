Public Talks

I have 9+ years of experience providing planetarium-based and open-air guided tours of the night's sky and I provide talks to general audiences of a range of ages on subjects including:

  • how stars and their planets form;
  • the Rosetta comet-landing mission;
  • the astronomical field of interferometry.

    • For bookings and enquiries, please use my contact details at the bottom of this page.
