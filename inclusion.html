Inclusion

Systemic inequalities exist within physics, astronomy, and the wider STEMM (science, technology, engineering, mathematics and medicine) sector which act as barriers to access to -- and progression through -- these disciplines for women, minority groups, and those with limited access to "science capital". I am committed to improving this situation through my work, scientific outreach activities and my active engagement in equity, diversity and inclusion work.

I provide talks or workshops on LGBTQ+ inclusion within STEMM, in particular, and speak at events focussed on improving the gender balance in STEMM. See this page for a list of events and activities I've recently been involved in.

For bookings and enquiries, please use my contact details at the bottom of this page.
